Signature Chat

Home / About

This site was conceived in July 2017. It is a simple chat app that allows for user drawn content. The brush size and color also randomly changes after each post you make.

To create a room simply enter your name and (optionally) a room name. That room will exist for 24 hours and can be accessed by anyone with the room code.

To chat you can either enter in text at the bottom of your screen or draw something. Everyone can see what's posted and everyone can see the users.
